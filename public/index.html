FAQ

The basics founders ask before working together.
Are you an outbound agency?

Outbound is the starting point because it creates leverage fast. The longer-term value is FounderOps: we stay embedded to keep priorities shipping across ops and fundraising.

How do you communicate?

Async-first. You get a weekly update memo with what shipped, what is blocked, and what we are doing next. Calls only when needed.

How fast can we start?

Typically within days, assuming access to the tools and context. We prioritize speed, but we do not skip fundamentals that will break later.

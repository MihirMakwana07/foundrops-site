FAQ

Honest answers upfront. Less friction later.

What if we already have a team?

Great. We plug into your existing workflow and take ownership of a lane (outbound ops, fundraising ops, founder ops). We also build SOPs so your team can keep it running.

Do you guarantee results?

No. Anyone who guarantees outcomes in outbound or fundraising is selling you a story. What we do guarantee is execution quality: clear inputs, clean tracking, consistent cadence, and honest reporting.

What do you need from us weekly?

One short weekly sync (or async check-in) plus decisions on the few items only you can decide. Everything else is handled through a shared tracker and updates.

Do you work async?

Yes. Async-first by default (Slack, Notion, email), with a light weekly sync to keep momentum.

What tools do you use?

Whatever is already working for you. Common stacks include Notion, Google Sheets, Airtable, and CRM tooling. The priority is clean tracking and simple handoffs.
